*** MANIPAL CAMPUS LORE & WORLD DATA ***

[LOCATIONS]
1. Tiger Circle (TC): The chaotic center. Auto-rickshaws swarm here like hornets.
2. Student Plaza (SP): The marble-floored social hub. Safe zone for resting.
3. MIT Food Court 1: The noisy, echoing hall of sustenance.
4. MIT Food Court 2 (FC2): The quieter, slightly more upscale dining experience.
5. End Point: A cliff-edge park. High wind gusts. Rumored to heal 'Mental Fatigue'.
6. Central Library: The fortress of silence. free AC, but forbids talking.
7. KMC Greens: The romantic PvP zone. Beware of flashlight-wielding guards at night.
8. Voody's: The burger sanctuary. Grants 'Grease Shield' buff.
9. DeeTee (The Ruins): An underground legend. Dimly lit.
10. Venugopal Temple: A zone of absolute peace. Clears negative status effects.
11. Canara Mall: The capitalist dungeon. High-tier loot sold here.
12. Hostel Block 10: The party fortress. High noise levels, low sleep.
13. Hostel Block 1-4 (The Old Blocks): Ancient structures. Spawns huge insects.
14. The Innovation Centre (IC): High-tech zone. Grants +5 Intelligence.
15. Marena Complex: The massive sports arena. Requires 'Membership Card' to enter.
16. The TAPMI Hill: A treacherous climb. Drains stamina rapidly.
17. Academic Block 1 (AB1): The maze of classrooms. easy to get lost.
18. Academic Block 5 (AB5): The grand towering structure. Home to the Director.
19. Kamath Circle (KC): A small hub with legendary tea shops.
20. Malpe Beach (Travel Zone): A fast-travel location. Good for morale, bad for attendance.
21. Kapu Lighthouse: Grants 'Vision' buff. Dangerous slippery rocks.
22. Delta Point: Where the river meets the sea. scenic but treacherous currents.
23. The Railway Bridge: A scenic spot for contemplation.
24. Syndicate Circle: The gateway to the town market.
25. Inox Manipal: The cinema dungeon. Time moves faster here.
26. Dollops: The American diner simulation. Expensive food.
27. Pangala: The morning breakfast shrine. Famous for idli-sambar.
28. Shai: The late-night tea spot. Spawns stressed engineers.
29. Coin Circle: A minor junction. Good for finding auto-rickshaws.
30. The Anatomy Museum: Filled with skeletons. Spooky vibes.
31. NIH (New International Hostel): The luxury zone. AC blast +10.

[ENEMIES & BOSSES]
1. The Auto Rickshaw Driver: Fast, aggressive. Special Move: "Meter Broken."
2. The Hostel Warden: Enforces curfew. Can cast 'Lockout'.
3. The Campus Monkey: Thief class. Steals food items from inventory.
4. The Internal Assessment (IA): Mini-boss. drains HP (Hope Points).
5. The Manipal Rain: Weather boss. Sudden, violent, destroys electronics.
6. The Proctor: Stealth boss. Catches players smoking or loitering.
7. The Strict Invigilator: Eagle-eyed. Prevents cheating or talking.
8. The Stray Dog Pack: roams at night. High damage in groups.
9. The Mess Worker: serves "Mystery Curry". Probability of poisoning.
10. The GPA Tank: A heavy boss that requires constant grinding to defeat.
11. The 8AM Lecture: Drains 'Sleep' stat. Hard to attend.
12. The Surprise Quiz: Appears without warning. High critical hit chance.
13. The External Examiner: The terrifying end-game boss of the lab exam.
14. The Backlog: A recurring ghost that haunts you until graduation.
15. The Humidity: Passive environment damage. Drains stamina constantly.
16. The Roommate's Alarm: Sonic attack. Disrupts rest.
17. The Assignment Deadline: A time-based bomb. Explodes if not defused.
18. The Auto Strike: Stops all fast travel. Forces player to walk.
19. The Power Cut: Plunges the world into darkness. Wi-Fi disabled.
20. The Slippery Moss: Environmental trap during monsoon.
21. The Library Guard: Confiscates ID cards if you make noise.
22. The Gym Bro at Marena: Intimidates players with low Strength stats.
23. The Senior Student: Can be an ally or a bully depending on Charisma.
24. The Fake Friend: Borrows money and never returns it.
25. The Lab Assistant: Rejects your records for bad handwriting.
26. The Bus Conductor: Shouts loudly. Induces headache.
27. The Flying Cockroach: A terrifying aerial enemy found in old hostels.
28. The Mold: Grows on leather items during monsoon. Destroys equipment.
29. The Slow Wi-Fi: Psychological torture. Lags your actions.
30. FINAL BOSS: The Director: Holds the Scroll of Graduation.

9. Bus Pass: Allows free travel on campus buses.
10. Maggi Noodles: Crafted item. Restores +5 HP. 2-minute craft time.
11. Ghee Roast: Rare consumable. +50 HP. Extremely expensive.
12. Filter Coffee: Clears 'Sleepy' status. +5 Agility.
13. Scientific Calculator: Weapon against Math exams.
14. Lab Coat: Light Armor. +2 Defense against chemical spills.
15. Drafter: Heavy weapon. Can be used as a club.
16. Laptop Charger: Lifeline item. Without it, Game Over.
17. Hostel Key: Don't lose this. Replacement costs 500 Gold.
18. Odomos Cream: Repels mosquitoes. Essential for evening study.
19. Iron Box: Forbidden item. contraband.
20. Kettle: Forbidden item. Used to cook Maggi.
21. Manipal Hoodie: Armor. +10 Comfort. +5 Social Status.
22. Cold Coffee from Juice Center: +10 Stamina.
23. Shawarma: Late night fuel. +15 HP.
24. Crocs: The official footwear of Manipal. +5 Speed in Rain.
25. Blue Book: Required for exams. Costs 5 Gold.
26. Prescription Glasses: +2 Perception.
27. Earphones: Blocks 'Noise' damage from roommates.
28. Backpack: Increases inventory size by 10 slots.
29. Power Bank: backup energy source.
30. The Degree Certificate: The Ultimate Legendary Item.

17. Bandh (Strike): Shops closed. Resource scarcity.

19. Farewell: Emotional damage.

21. Room Inspection: Stealth mission. Hide contraband immediately.
22. Water Shortage: Survival mode activated.
23. Internet Outage: Mass panic event.
24. midnight Canteen Opening: Rare merchant spawns.

29. Graduation Day: The credits roll.
